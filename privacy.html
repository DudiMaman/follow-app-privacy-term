Privacy Policy for FollowApp

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FollowApp ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the FollowApp mobile application (the "App") on iOS and Android.

By using FollowApp, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. About FollowApp

FollowApp helps you track public Instagram profiles — view follower and following counts, post counts, profile changes, and receive alerts about activity on profiles you choose to track.

FollowApp is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Instagram or Meta Platforms, Inc. We only access publicly available Instagram profile information. We never ask for your Instagram username, password, or login credentials.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Instagram usernames you search or track: When you look up a profile or add it to your tracked list, the username is processed by our systems to retrieve publicly available profile data from Instagram.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous User Identifier: When you install FollowApp, we generate a random anonymous ID for your installation. We do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number, and we do not require account creation.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, language, and country/region.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you enable notifications, your device's push token is stored so we can deliver alerts (e.g., when a tracked profile changes).
  • Usage Data: Screens viewed, taps, in-app interactions, paywall views, plan selections, and other product analytics events.
  • Session Replay Data: We record anonymized sessions (interactions with the app interface) to diagnose issues and improve usability. Sensitive inputs are masked where applicable.
  • Diagnostics: Network telemetry, error/crash data, and performance information.

2.3 Purchase Information

When you subscribe to FollowApp Premium, your purchase is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We do receive, via our subscription processor (RevenueCat):

  • Subscription status, plan type, trial status, renewal/expiration dates
  • Anonymized transaction identifiers and receipts
  • Country of purchase

2.4 Information About Third-Party Profiles

When you search or track an Instagram profile, FollowApp retrieves publicly available information that Instagram makes accessible without authentication, including:

  • Username, display name, profile picture, biography
  • Follower count, following count, post count
  • Verified status, account category, external link
  • Whether the account is public or private

We do not collect private posts, direct messages, or any content that requires logging into Instagram to view.

2.5 What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: contact info, name, email, phone number, physical address, payment card data, precise or coarse location, health data, financial information beyond purchase records, contacts list, photos, videos, audio, messages, browsing history outside the app, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate core App functionality, including retrieving tracked Instagram profile data
  • Deliver push notifications about profile changes you've opted into
  • Process subscriptions, manage trial periods, and prevent payment fraud
  • Analyze usage to improve features, fix bugs, and measure performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for third-party advertising, do not sell your personal information, and do not share it with data brokers.

5. Instagram Data and Third-Party Sources

FollowApp retrieves publicly available data from Instagram's public profile endpoints. We are not affiliated with Instagram or Meta. Your use of FollowApp does not create any account on Instagram and does not share your personal information with Instagram.

The accuracy, completeness, and timeliness of Instagram profile data depend on what Instagram publicly exposes. Some data may be cached on your device or our servers temporarily to improve performance.

6. Data Retention

  • On-device data (tracked profile list, preferences, free-lookup counters): retained until you delete the App or clear App data.
  • Push notification tokens: retained for as long as you have the App installed with notifications enabled, and deleted when you uninstall or disable notifications.
  • Subscription records (via RevenueCat): retained for the lifetime of the subscription and for legal/accounting purposes thereafter.
  • Analytics and session replay data (via PostHog): retained according to PostHog's default retention (typically up to 12 months) unless required longer to investigate abuse or comply with law.
  • Instagram username search logs: retained only as long as needed to operate the lookup; aggregated, non-personal usage statistics may be retained longer.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the legal basis)

Because FollowApp does not require an account, you can exercise most rights directly:

  • Delete all on-device data: uninstall the App or use your device's "Clear App Data" option
  • Stop receiving notifications: disable notifications in iOS/Android settings
  • Cancel your subscription: manage subscriptions in Apple App Store or Google Play
  • Request deletion of server-side data: contact us at the address below

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information, and to non-discrimination. We do not sell or share personal information for targeted advertising.

EEA, UK, and Swiss Residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Our legal bases for processing are: (a) performance of a contract (delivering app features you request), (b) legitimate interests (improving the App, preventing fraud, securing our systems), and (c) consent (for push notifications and optional analytics, where required). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any right, contact us at the email in Section 12.

8. Children's Privacy

FollowApp is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK, where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

The App is rated for users 13+ on the App Store and Google Play.
